🔐 Privacy Policy
for Dope War

📅 Last updated: March 28, 2026

Dope War is an ad‑supported mobile game built with simplicity and fun in mind. This page explains how we handle any data when you use our app. We take your privacy seriously – no tricky stuff.

📱 Information Collection & Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you directly. All game progress (high scores, settings) stays on your device and is never sent to us. The app uses third‑party services that may collect certain information to help the app work (like showing ads or fixing bugs).

🤝 Third‑Party Services

To keep the game free, we use Google AdMob for advertising. These services may collect data about your device to serve relevant ads and improve performance. Their privacy policies are linked below:

  • 🔹 Google Play Services (including Google Play Games)
  • 🔹 Google AdMob (advertising partner)

We also use standard Android / iOS system features. No personal data is sold or shared beyond what these third‑party services require.

📊 Log Data

When an error occurs in the app, third‑party tools (like Google Play Console or AdMob) may collect log data. This can include your device’s IP address, device name, operating system version, and app settings. This helps us fix crashes and improve stability.

🍪 Cookies / Local Storage

The game itself does not use traditional cookies. However, Google AdMob may use cookies or similar technologies to show you ads that are more relevant. You can learn how to manage ad preferences through your device’s settings or Google’s ad controls.

🧒 Children’s Privacy

Our app is not aimed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal info, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who have concerns can contact us using the email below.
